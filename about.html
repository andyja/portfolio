All about me.....

Hey, I'm Andy. For the last 12 or so months I've been learning to code. I live in Leeds with my partner, two children and dog called MarceloBielsa. In 2020 I had to give up my sales job to open a home school. In doing so I decided a life on the road was no longer for me.

I started my career working in kitchens at 14, gradually making my way to being a head chef. I was then recruited by a supplier to sell equipment to other chefs. I loved this and was good at it, later moving to a more specialised role selling the most technologically advanced wine glasses. In this role I completed WSET's gruelling Diploma, forging friendships and contacts along the way.
